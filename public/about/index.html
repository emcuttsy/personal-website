Hi, I'm Elise.

I’m a first-year geobiology PhD student at MIT in the Bosak Group. My scientific interests are broadly in the ongoing and historical interactions between life and the Earth, especially when it comes to microbial life and Earth’s surface environmental conditions.

Before MIT, I studied geobiology at Caltech and spent a Fulbright involved in geobiological research at the University of Southern Denmark in Odense, Denmark.

Thanks for visiting!
